# Funding & Withdrawals

You fund your account by connecting a self-custodial wallet and depositing supported assets: BTC, ETH, USDT, and BNB.

Withdrawals are sent back to your connected wallet and typically settle within 24 hours. BlackQuant charges no withdrawal fees — you only pay the network gas fee. The minimum withdrawal is the equivalent of $10.

Because BlackQuant is non-custodial and keeps a small reserve buffer for open positions, the "Available to Withdraw" figure on the Treasury page can be lower than your "Total Balance".

If a withdrawal is stuck in "Pending" for more than 24 hours, open a ticket with human support from the Help Desk — the assistant can't access your account.
