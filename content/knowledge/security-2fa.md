# Security, 2FA & Verification

BlackQuant is non-custodial: your private keys never leave your wallet, and the trading bot has execution permission only — it can place trades but can never move or withdraw your funds.

**Two-factor authentication (2FA):** enable it under Personal → 2FA. BlackQuant supports authenticator apps using time-based one-time passwords (TOTP), such as Google Authenticator or Authy. SMS 2FA is not supported.

**Verification:** completing identity verification under Personal → Verification raises your withdrawal limits and unlocks higher subscription tiers.

BlackQuant will never ask for your seed phrase, private key, or 2FA code. If anyone does, it is a scam — report it from the Help Desk.
