# Signal Plans

BlackQuant offers three subscription tiers for automated trade signals:

- **Starter — $29/mo**: up to 10 signals/day, 1 active position, email alerts, basic analytics.
- **Growth — $79/mo**: up to 50 signals/day, 5 active positions, SMS & email alerts, advanced analytics, referral rewards.
- **Elite — $199/mo**: unlimited signals and positions, priority alert routing, full analytics suite, a dedicated account manager, and API access.

Choosing annual billing saves 20% versus monthly.

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle; upgrades apply immediately and are prorated. A plan stays inactive until you fund your account.
